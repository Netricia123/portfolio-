Squad Up

Overview

SquadUp is a privacy-first video-sharing platform designed for users who want the creativity of TikTok without the pressure of a global stage. It allows users to form “Squads”, private micro-communities where videos and reels are shared only with trusted members.

Based on user settings, content can “ascend” from a private squad to a public view, reaching users with similar interests.

Why SquadUp?

Many social media users are ghost users. They enjoy content but are too shy to post their own because they don’t know who might be watching. SquadUp eliminates this audience anxiety by ensuring total user distribution sovereignty.
